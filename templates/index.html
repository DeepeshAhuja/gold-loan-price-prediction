Various Schemes that we offer:

1. Agricultural Jewel Loan:

  • Purpose: To meet Short-term production credit needs of farmers
  • Limit: Maximum limit per borrower Rs. 10 lakhs
  • Repayment: Bullet payment in one year

2. Agriculture Term Loan - Jewellery:

  • Purpose: to meet Investment credit needs of farmers
  • Limit: Maximum Limit Per Borrower Rs. 25 Lakhs
  • Repayment: Maximum 3 years in monthly/Quarterly/Half yearly/Yearly

3. Jewel Loan SME:

  • Purpose: to meet investment need in MSME - Manufacturing, Services and Retail Trade sectors
  • Limit: Maximum limit per borrower of Rs. 5 lakhs
  • Repayment: Bullet payment in one year

4. Jewel Loan - Others:

  • Purpose: to meet domestic/business needs
  • Limit: Maximum limit per borrower of Rs. 25 lakhs
  • Repayment: For loan upto 5 Lacs -bullet payment in one year and for loan above 5 lacs-in monthly EMIs with maximum 35 months

5. IOB Swarnalakshmi:

  • Purpose: To meet domestic needs of Women customers (i.e. non-productive purpose), who is having saving/ current Account with us
  • Age: Minimum 18 year
  • Limit:: Maximum limit per borrower of Rs. 5.00 Lakhs
  • Repayment: Loan to be repaid within a period of 12 months as bullet payment - both principal and interest due for payment at the end of the term
  • Free Insurance: A free accidental insurance cover of Rs.2 Lacs under PMSBY to individual women beneficiaries for First time.
